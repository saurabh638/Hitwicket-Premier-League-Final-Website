Rules & FAQs

General Questions

Hitwicket Premier League (HPL) 2025 is a massive community-driven esports cricket tournament where players vote for cities, elect head coaches, participate in live player auctions, and compete in tournament matches. It's the ultimate cricket gaming experience combining strategy, community engagement, and competitive gameplay.

HPL 2025 runs from November 14, 2025 to January 31, 2026. The event is divided into multiple phases including city voting, coach elections, player auctions, and tournament matches.

All registered Hitwicket players at Level 10 or above are eligible to participate in HPL 2025.

During the City Voting phase (November 14-17), visit the HPL website, log in with your Hitwicket account, and vote for your favorite city. Each account gets one vote.

No, votes are final once submitted. Choose carefully as you cannot modify your vote after submission.

During the Head Coach Nominations phase (November 19-21), eligible players can submit their nomination through the provided form. You can nominate yourself to represent your chosen city.

Tournament Rules

  • Players must be Level 10+ to participate
  • One vote per account per phase
  • Votes are final and cannot be modified
  • Franchises must follow naming guidelines
  • Players selected must be in-game characters
  • All decisions final as per organizer discretion
  • Fair play and sportsmanship required
  • No multi-accounting allowed
